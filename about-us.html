About Our Company

We Buy Bad Properties is a company focused on helping clients who own “bad” properties i.e. properties in distress, sell those properties and earn cash. What this means is that we buy houses in their current state (i.e. no repairs needed), will pay cash, and then will handle all closing costs in a timely fashion.

About Joe

Since 2002, Joseph L. Grimaldi has been a Real Estate Bank Attorney and a member of the Real Estate Bar Association of Massachusetts. Grimaldi is a long time real estate investor who specializes in buying “bad” properties.
